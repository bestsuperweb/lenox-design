Residences

Experience luxurious, carefree living the moment you enter Lenox Terrace. Our white-gloved doormen and attentive staff are at your service around the clock to ensure your comfort and safety.

History

The first of the six Lennox Terrace buildings was completed in 1958, in the centrally located cultural epicenter of Harlem.

As one of the city’s first urban renewal projects, Lenox Terrace has remained family owned and operated by The Olnick Organization. After nearly 60 years, these world-class apartments are still recognized as one of the area's finest residences. This premier address has been home to many respected politicians, entertainers, and business executives.
Living Spaces

The Lenox Terrace apartments offer sophisticated living spaces complemented by modern accents and warm hardwood floors.

Each room in finished with a chic and minimalist color scheme. Enjoy the large windows with picturesque views that draw in natural sunlight. Deluxe-sized closets are featured throughout each apartment.
Kitchens

Whether you're cooking or entertaining, you’ll enjoy the simplified layout of these well appointed professional kitchens.

From the abundant counter space to the premium cabinets, there is plenty of storage. Even the novice chef will appreciate the premium stainless steel appliances.
Bathrooms

For a spa-like feel, the bathrooms are tastefully appointed with fine tile work that complements any decor.

The shower/soaking-tub combination features an oversized showerhead. Modern amenities and fixtures create an atmosphere of extravagance.
